Pricing Simulator

Ethical Yoga App — interactive income-based pricing engine with scaling teacher rate

Tier Ratios

teacher rate = min rate + (target rate − min rate) × (students − min) / (max − min)
student price = (room + teacher rate) / sum of ratios × student's ratio
